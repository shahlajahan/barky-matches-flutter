## Platform Value

PetSupo is not a simple pet profile app. It combines discovery, booking, business dashboards, medical records, marketplace flows, adoption, social features, and safety tools in one platform.

## Monetization Areas

- Business subscriptions
- Appointment commissions
- Marketplace commissions
- Featured listings
- Advertising
- Premium pet owner features
- Pet taxi commissions
- NGO / CSR partnerships

## Social Impact Areas

- Adoption support
- Lost & found recovery
- Green Memorial
- Community safety
- Responsible pet ownership
